# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Prioritizing community outcomes over individual preference

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and abuse.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code. Reports may be sent to maintainers via repository security/contact channels.
